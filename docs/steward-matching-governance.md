# Steward AI × Steward Human Governance

## Authority boundary

- A Steward AI may research, recommend, and draft only within its active Passport permissions.
- A Steward Human owns the final decision and remains accountable for external effects.
- Payment, contract, identity verification, and external communication require a separate Human approval record.
- AP2 mandate scope never expands the execution permission recorded in the Agent Passport.
- `dry_run` is the only supported MVP mode.

## Lifecycle

`Academy → Passport → supervised practice → level matching → outcome evidence → retraining`

Levels are Candidate, Junior, Professional, Senior, and Lead. Education fees and certification decisions must be institutionally separated. Junior work requires an identified supervisor and cannot perform external actions.
